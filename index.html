Peanut Butter & Jelly

Sandwich

I love Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwiches. My favorite peanut butter is Peter Pan's Creamy Peanut Butter. I perfer to use Welches' Grape Jelly. White bread goes best with this sandwich, but you can choose which type of bread you want to use.

Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 slices of bread

Steps:

  1. Remove 2 slices of bread from the package
  2. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Bring the two slices of bread together
